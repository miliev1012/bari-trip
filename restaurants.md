## Must-Try Foods

- **Focaccia barese** — fluffy olive oil flatbread with tomatoes
- **Orecchiette con le cime di rapa** — iconic Puglian pasta
- **Panzerotto** — fried/baked dough stuffed with mozzarella & tomato
- **Taralli** — crunchy ring-shaped snack with fennel seeds
- **Burrata di Andria** — the creamiest burrata you'll ever taste
- **Spaghetti all'assassina** — spicy pan-fried spaghetti, a Bari specialty

---

## Dining Tips

- Lunch: typically 12:30–2:30 PM · Dinner: starts around 7:30–8 PM
- Arrive at opening (12:00 or 7:30) for shorter waits
- Italian restaurants are very child-friendly — ask for plain pasta or half-portions
- Always book **La Cantina dello Zio** ahead of time (only 2 days/week!)
- Have cash — smaller places may not take cards
