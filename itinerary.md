# Itinerary

## Day 1 — Saturday, March 28 · Arrival

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Arrival | Pick up rental car at Bari Airport (Sicily by Car counter) |
| Morning/Afternoon | Jérôme Cafè — pastries, cappuccino (open daily 7 AM–9 PM) |
| After 3 PM | Check-in: Il Leon D'Oro (luggage storage available before check-in) |
| Evening | Lungomare Araldo di Crollalanza — flat seafront promenade, stroller-friendly |
| Dinner | **Antò Ristorante** — authentic Puglian cuisine, orecchiette, ragu (open from 11:45 AM) |
| After dinner | Via Sparano da Bari — pick up water & snacks (shops open until 8 PM) |

---

## Day 2 — Sunday, March 29 · Zoosafari Day

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 7:30 AM | Breakfast: Melo Coffee and Bakery (Sun 7:30 AM–2:30 PM) |
| 9:00 AM | Depart for Zoosafari Fasano (1 hr drive via SS16) |
| 10 AM–2 PM | **Zoosafari Fasanolandia** — arrive at opening! Drive-through safari, lemur walk (+€3), rides |
| Lunch | Inside the park or packed snacks |
| ~3 PM | Drive back to Bari — nap time in the car |
| Afternoon | Old Town stroll + mini-market for water and snacks |
| Dinner | **Le Nicchie** — spaghetti all'assassina, seafood pasta (Sun 11:30 AM–3:30 PM & 7:30–11 PM) |

> Bring carrots for animal feeding at Zoosafari. Buy tickets online at zoosafari.it (~€140 family).

---

## Day 3 — Monday, March 30 · Bari Old Town

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning | Breakfast: Melo Coffee and Bakery (Mon–Sat 7 AM–9 PM) |
| Morning | **Basilica di San Nicola** — free entry, resting place of St. Nicholas (daily 6:30 AM–8:30 PM) |
| Morning | **Cathedral of Saint Sabinus** — €9 combined ticket (daily 8:30 AM–7 PM) |
| Lunch | **Mastro Ciccio** — focaccia barese, panzerotti, ~€12–15/person (open from 10 AM daily) |
| Afternoon | Stroller walk along Lungomare for nap time |
| Afternoon snack | **Il Caffettaio** — best café in Bari, macchiato & pastries (Mon–Fri 7 AM–5 PM) |
| Afternoon | **Largo Albicocca (Orecchiette Alley)** — watch nonnas make fresh pasta by hand |
| Evening | Via Sparano / ZARA — shopping and restocking |
| Dinner | See restaurants.md — La Cantina dello Zio only open Wed & Thu, plan accordingly |

---

## Day 4 — Tuesday, March 31 · Polignano a Mare

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning | Breakfast: Melo Coffee and Bakery |
| 10 AM | Drive to Polignano a Mare (~30 min via SS16 coastal road) |
| Morning | **Lama Monachile** — iconic cliff cove, crystal water (bring beach shoes, rocky!) |
| Lunch | Old town seafood restaurant — ask locals for recommendation |
| Afternoon | Drive back to Bari or find shaded piazza for stroller nap |
| Afternoon snack | Jérôme Cafè — open daily 7 AM–9 PM |
| Afternoon | Old Town wandering + Arco delle Meraviglie |
| Evening | Book dinner in the afternoon! |
| Dinner | **Antò Ristorante** or **Le Nicchie** (both open Tues) |
| Evening | Last shopping — Via Sparano / ZARA / Supermarket, souvenirs & travel snacks |

---

## Day 5 — Wednesday, April 1 · Checkout & Departure

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 7 AM | Final breakfast: Melo Coffee and Bakery |
| 11 AM | Check-out from Il Leon D'Oro (luggage storage available) |
| Optional | **Alberobello** — Trulli Rione Monti, go 8–10 AM to beat crowds (~1 hr drive) |
| Lunch | In Alberobello or back in Bari |
| Afternoon | Return rental car at Bari Airport (allow extra time from Alberobello) |

> La Cantina dello Zio is only open Wed & Thu — could fit a dinner here if departure is late enough.
