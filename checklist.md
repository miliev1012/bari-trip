## Before You Go

- [ ] Book **La Cantina dello Zio** — only open Wed & Thu, call +39 329 229 9403
- [ ] Buy **Zoosafari tickets** online at zoosafari.it (~€140 family ticket, saves time at gate)

## Packing

- [ ] Stroller + rain cover (end of March can be rainy)
- [ ] Beach shoes / sandals (essential for Polignano — Lama Monachile is rocky)
- [ ] Light jacket (12–18°C in late March)
- [ ] Cash (smaller places may not take cards)
- [ ] Snacks & water bottle for day trips
